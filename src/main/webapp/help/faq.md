## What data is included?
scSAID aggregates single-cell RNA-seq datasets from skin and appendage tissues across species, conditions, and anatomical regions. Each dataset includes curated metadata, clustering annotations, and supporting analysis outputs.

## How often is the database updated?
Updates are released as new datasets are validated and processed through the standardized pipeline. The update cadence may vary based on data availability and validation steps.

## How do I cite scSAID?
Please cite the scSAID project page and the original data sources when using datasets or figures in publications. A recommended citation format will be added here.

## Why can't I see a dataset?
Some datasets may be temporarily hidden while they are being reprocessed or validated. If you need access, please contact the team with the dataset identifier.
